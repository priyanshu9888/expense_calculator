About SplitLedger

A fast, browser-based shared expense manager built for groups who want fair, transparent bill splitting.

Our purpose

We want to make group expenses simple by eliminating manual calculations and confusion. SplitLedger helps groups split costs quickly and fairly.

How it works

Add members, log shared items, record who paid, and generate a settlement plan in seconds.

Privacy first

Your data stays in the browser. No account is needed and no data is stored on a server.
